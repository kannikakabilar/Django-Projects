    About Us

    The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) - University of Toronto powered by Google Developers is a student-led group that aims to inspire students in the field of technology. Our community is open to any student in any discipline wishing to advance their skills in developer technology and no prior developer experience is required to be a part. We provide workshops, events and hands-on projects that support and empower all university students who are passionate about growing as a developer to solve real-time problems and increase their knowledge of developer products.
